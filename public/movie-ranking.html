The Best Movies According to Zarifa

My top 3 movies of all-time

Godfather

Francis Ford Coppola’s The Godfather is a cinematic masterpiece.

Oldboy

Park Chan-wook’s Oldboy is a brutal, stylish, and emotionally intense thriller.

Fargo

The Coen Brothers’ Fargo is a darkly comic crime drama that blends quirky small-town charm with chilling violence.
